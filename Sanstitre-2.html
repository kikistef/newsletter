Impact of Bitcoin Spot ETFs on the Cryptocurrency Market

Since their introduction in January, Bitcoin Spot ETFs have captured a significant portion of the Bitcoin market, amassing 776,464 bitcoins valued at $47.7 billion in just two months. This shift towards institutional investment has bolstered Bitcoin's price and legitimacy, drawing a wider investor base.
